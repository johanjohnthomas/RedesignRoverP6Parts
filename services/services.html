Our Services

We offer a variety of services for RoverP6 cars.

Machine & Sleeving

As well as New Parts for P6’s we also refurbish calipers and brake servos. Rear calipers have the hydraulic cylinder machined out and sleeved with stainless steel and this also applies to brake servo valve bodies. Get in touch if you have any questions.

Refurbishing

Other refurbished parts include water pumps for Rover 2000 & 2200 both short and long nose and also the Rover 3500.

Chroming

We have the facility to re-chrome most items for example, bumpers, number plate plinths, overriders, fuel filler caps, wheel nuts, radiator cowls, rocker cover dome nuts, pretty much anything.

Polishing

The most recent addition to our range is polished rocker covers than make your engine bays gleam.
