We have over 30 years of experience of owning, repairing, restoring and running Rover P6 cars at all variants. 200, 2200 & 3500, saloon and estate, both as everyday and show condition cars. Our current fleet includes an everyday user 1966 Rover 2000, and also a 1972 Rover 2000TC export, 1975 Rover 2200 Auto ,1972 Rover 3500 Estoura (estate) and of course the last P6 off the production line 'VVC 700S', the avocardo Rover 3500S. We have over 25 years of involvement in classic cars club mostly at comittee level and have now turned our specialist knowledge and experience into our Rover P6 built between 1963-1977. The parts stocked are predonminantly brand new manufactured items but there is also a mix of NOS - new old stock- refurbished services exchange and second hand parts.
